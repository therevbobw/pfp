The Bench

A place within the park,
but looking beyond the park,
to the street,
houses and the hills.

God we thank you
for little spaces of time
carved out from busy days;
spaces of quiet and of remembering.
We pray for travellers on the road,
for their safety,
and for good in their encounters today.
We pray your peace
on every household here.
We thank you for the hills and sky,
and ask your help to keep them safe.
