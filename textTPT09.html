A Gap in the Trees

Sometimes we see in fits and starts.
And, to be honest, God,
I don't like it.
I wish it were always clear
where I was and what I had to do.
But even Jesus didn't always know:
I remember his desperate prayer
in the garden, for the cup of suffering
to be taken away.

God, lead us, we pray:
help us to know at least,
the next step.
