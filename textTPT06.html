Torside Crossing

Here is the place where
power and vulnerability meet,
the motor road crosses the foot road.
And when power and vulnerability meet
we pray for justice, fairness,
and something more generous:
we pray for mercy.
May speeds on the road be slow:
may little children in pink cycle hats
peep safely round the railings
and cross the road in one piece.

We remember Jesus,
led like a lamb to the slaughter,
man without power in that moment,
who knows what it is
to be vulnerable.
God, today,
save all who are vulnerable.
