Hadfield Station

We pray for each person
who uses this station;
for those who escape paying,
for the people going to the City game,
for commuters, for walkers,
for shoppers,
for old and young,
for good and bad:
they are all loved by you:
show us how to pray for them,
how to hope for all good things
to come to them.

We thank you for all who work
on the railway,
and ask you to keep them safe.
